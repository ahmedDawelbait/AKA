About Us

With a tracked record of work in the middle east for 10 years, Eng. Maram Seddiq's practice has covered all types of projects; from residential to governmental, in addition to retail and hospitality.

Maram uses fundamentals to create innovative ideas.

Perfection in proportions characterizes her practice, aiming to achieve ultimate purity and functionality that creates a field of positive energy.

Her philosophy is to complement the natural organic beauty by adding her distinguished human touch of straight lines, sharp angles and clear axis making her work remarkably bold, especially when utilizing strong materials that grant her work eternity.
